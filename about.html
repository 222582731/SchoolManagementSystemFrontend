About Our System

The School Management System is a web-based platform designed to simplify academic and administrative processes in schools. It centralizes student enrollment, attendance, grade management, timetables, and communication, making education more efficient and accessible.

Our Goals

  • Automate manual processes like attendance and grade tracking.
  • Improve communication between students, lecturers, and coordinators.
  • Provide real-time access to academic and administrative information.
  • Ensure secure storage and management of student data.

Stakeholders

The system is designed for:

  • Students: Access grades, timetables, and course materials.
  • Lecturers: Manage grades, attendance, and communicate with students.
  • Coordinators: Oversee modules and handle student queries.
  • Dean: Ensure system aligns with university goals and performance.

Benefits

  • Save time for staff and lecturers by automating routine tasks.
  • Enhance student learning through easy access to resources.
  • Enable informed decision-making with real-time data.
  • Improve academic performance tracking and communication.
